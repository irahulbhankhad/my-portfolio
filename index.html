About Me

I am an aspiring Data Analyst who focuses on turning raw data into clear, actionable insights for real-world business problems.

I hold an M.Sc in Mathematics from IIT Jodhpur and a B.Sc in Computer Science from Kurukshetra University, which gives me a strong foundation in logic, problem-solving, and analytical thinking.

I work primarily with SQL, Python, Excel, and Tableau, and I enjoy building clean dashboards and writing efficient queries to support data-driven decisions.

I approach data analytics with a problem-solving mindset — I try, fail, debug, and improve. This hands-on process has helped me develop strong query logic, dashboard design skills, and the ability to translate numbers into meaningful stories.
